Elements definitions

h1
"Header 1"
Creates a large main header.
h2
"Header 2"
Creates a sub header.
h3
"Header 3"
Creates a sub-sub header.
p
"Paragraph"
Creates a paragraph for text.
ul
"Unordered List"
Creates a bulleted list without numbers.
ol
"Ordered List"
Creates a numbered list for items.
li
"Listed Item"
Adds an item to a list.
