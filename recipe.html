Cookie Recipe

Here is some of the stuff you will need to bake cookies.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Cookie Dough
  • Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • Milk

Steps to Bake it

  1. Mix ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Place cookies on baking sheet
  3. Set oven to 400 degrees. Let bake for 13 minutes.
  4. Take out of oven. You are done.
